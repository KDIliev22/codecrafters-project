Robotics & AI
- The fire of the New Age -


In this age of the 21st century we use ICT, Robotics, and A.I. in our daily lives. These technologies are very widely adopted across many different industries (e.g. Medicine, Manufacturing, R&D, etc.,). Regardless of what time has to say, tools powered by Artificial Intelligence are going to take around at least 20% – 30% of job opportunities. A.I. is going to be replacing people. In the automobile industry, automotive companies like Tesla, Mercedes, and Ford all have AI-powered systems which make their cars drive autonomously. The development & training of Artificial Intelligence is very high-priority in a lot of Big Pharma and Big Tech companies (e. g. Moderna, IBM, Microsoft, nVIDIA, Alphabet)...

Boston Dynamics is a critically acclaimed research and development company that has gained worldwide recognition for its groundbreaking advancements in robotics and automation. OpenAI

OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research laboratory and company that focuses on developing and promoting the use of advanced AI technology. It was founded in December 2015 by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, John Schulman, and Wojciech Zaremba. One of the primary goals of OpenAI is to ensure that artificial general intelligence (AGI) benefits all of humanity.
